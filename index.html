My Story

I am currently employed as a Senior Front End Developer at Nelnet, Inc. I live in Eau Claire, WI with my husband, three boys, two dogs, and a partridge in a pear tree.

I worked for 13 years at a weekly newspaper as a graphic designer and desktop publisher. When we started publishing our newspaper online, I was chosen for the task, as I had more technical skills than anyone else there. I soon realized that I wanted to know how it worked behind the scenes, so I signed up for classes at the technical college in Eau Claire. I graduated with an Associates Degree in IT-Software Development in 2015.

I interned as a UI/UX Designer at Great Lakes Higher Education Corporation, Inc. (now Nelnet, Inc.) in the summer of 2015. In the fall, I worked in the Internet marketing department at Mason Companies, Inc., as a web designer. When a front-end developer position opened up at Great Lakes, I contacted my former boss and she hooked me up. A little over a year after I started full-time, I was promoted to Senior Front-End Developer.
